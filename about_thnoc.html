ABOUT

I was lead designer on an urban exploration experience for the Historic New Orleans Collection, the premminent architectural research organization in the French Quarter. Visitors are able to browse the Collection's database of historic sites on a 3D architectural model of the French Quarter, and assemble "favorite" destinations that can then be transferred to a dedicated mobile app. The app then lets visitors go on their own walking tour of their collected destinations in the French Quarter.

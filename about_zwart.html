ABOUT

I had the privilege of doing production design and fabrication for "Zilly's War: Chapter One" a proof-of-concept science fiction short film from Reflection Films telling the story of Eve Abbadon, an astronaut on a multi-year mission to a close exoplanet called ZL-24971, a.k.a, Zilly. I designed and decorated sets for the Virgil space station, designed and built interactive props, created on-set UI motion graphics, and directed the overall aesthetic language of the film. The film was done on a micro-budget, and came together with only a small team of roughly 5 filmmakers that all contributed to various aspects of the production.

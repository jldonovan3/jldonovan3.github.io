ABOUT

I was design and narrative lead on the Albuquerque BioPark's newest exhibition, an state-of-the-art indoor sub-Antarctic penguin habitat with accompanying exhibit spaces. Visitors embark on their own research expedition to the Antarctic Peninsula, learning along the way about climate change, penguin ecoloy, and STEM concepts related to sub-Antarctic research. Multiple interactive exhibits allow visitors to talk to real-life contributing scientists about their work in the field, gather dynamic data points for other research vessels, steer a virtual penguin through its feeding grounds while avoiding obstacles, and scanning a replica ice core to learn about climate science.

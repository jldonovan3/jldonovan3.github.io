About Me

I helped design the user interaction paradigm and user interface for "Exploring Pueblo Pottery," a research project from Ideum where visitors view projection-mapped native designs on a 4' diameter water pot. I had the privilege of working with native Acoma artist Michelle Lowden in bringing her stunning designs into the digital realm, and telling the remarkable story of how native pottery designs constitute a form of written language that has endured for centuries.

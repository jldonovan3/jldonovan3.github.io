ABOUT

I helped design the user interface look and feel for a new series of exhibits in the Smithsonian's Moving Beyond Earth gallery at the National Air and Space Museum in Washington, D.C. I was also lead on redesigning the "Flight Director" interactive experience, where visitors become part of Mission Control for a critical Shuttle docking operation, and attempt to align their own decisions with that of the real-life flight director, Al Pennington.
